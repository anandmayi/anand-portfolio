About

I work in two directions at once. As Content Strategist at Inlite Media, a marketing agency I've been building from scratch in Gaya since January 2025, I write and strategize for Tier 2/3 businesses — real estate, hospitality, automotive, food — on the bet that they win on identity and trust, not budget or speed.

As Publishing Manager Intern at Notion Press, Chennai, I work directly with authors to take manuscripts from concept to publication, having shepherded 100+ book projects so far. Before that, I spent three months on the other side of the desk in Online Reputation Management, hearing directly from authors about what actually worked post-launch.

I hold an MA in Journalism and Mass Communication from SRMIST (2026), and a BCA in Computer Science from Magadh University before that — which is part of why I end up as the person translating between editorial judgment and the more technical, data-driven side of content: AI model evaluation, chatbot training, performance assessment.

Underneath the job titles, I've been a working writer since 2019 — book reviews, freelance pieces, radio production, a Routledge-affiliated academic paper, and enough documentary reviews (180+, for the IDSFFK Kerala film festival) to have strong opinions about pacing.
