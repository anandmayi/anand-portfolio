About

I work in two directions at once. As Content Strategist at Inlite Media, a marketing agency I've been building from scratch in Gaya since January 2025, I write and strategize for Tier 2/3 businesses — real estate, hospitality, automotive, food — on the bet that they win on identity and trust, not budget or speed.

As Ex Publishing Manager Intern at Notion Press, Chennai, I worked directly with authors to take manuscripts from concept to publication, having shepherded 100+ book projects so far. Before that, I spent three months on the other side of the desk in Online Reputation Management, hearing directly from authors about what actually worked post-launch.

When I'm not deep into anything, I'm either reading, scrolling the web, or talking randomly.

Underneath the job titles, I've been a working writer since 2019 — book reviews, freelance pieces.
